About Us

Hello and Welcome! Kaiser Carpentry is based in Pasadena, California. Our typical services include design and fabrication of custom carpentry, restoration, and repairs of residential type construction. We have been serving the local community for over 35 years.

It has been our privilege to support efforts to restore several city landmarks and many private residences alike. Twice we have been recognized by the City of Pasadena's Historic Preservation Commission for our participation in local restoration projects.

We are proud to be a part this community and to follow in the long line of craftsmen who built it. We enjoy challenges and look forward to working with you.
